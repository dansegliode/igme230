NMD ID Package

INTRO

The prompt for this animation package was to create an ID package for the New Media Design program at RIT. It includes a title sequence, animated bug, lower third, bumper, and end credits.

CONCEPT

My idea was to take different elements of new media like ui, web, 3d, and motion, and have all the parts work together like a rube goldberg machine to make the logo, similar to how all our areas of focus and our hard work make new media what it is.

MOOD & STYLE

I wanted the overall mood to be busy- multiple reactions happening at once, to reflect the often chaotic nature of our major.
