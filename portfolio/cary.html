The Cary Collection

The Cary Graphic Arts Collection is an archive of graphic communication history. It is located in the RIT library, and houses 40,000 volumes and manuscripts.

Their website is dated- at 10 years old it lacks accessible content and turns students away rather than engage them. Steven and Amelia, the curators of the collection, were the clients for the redesign of their site.

For this project, I used Adobe Illustrator, After Effects, and Invision.
