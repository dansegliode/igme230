The Cary Collection

INTRO

The Cary Graphic Arts Collection is an archive of graphic communication history. It is located in the RIT library, and houses 40,000 volumes and manuscripts.

ANALYSIS

Their website is dated- at 10 years old it lacks accessible content and turns students away rather than engage them. Steven and Amelia, the curators of the collection, were the clients for the redesign of their site.

WIREFRAMES

I wanted the feel of the site to be classy and still reflect the collection space, yet modernize the layout to show that it isn't outdated.

FINAL COMPS

A simple, elegant, and modern design is what the site needed to completely transform. With the redesign, users can easily navigate the site on desktop and mobile, whether they are looking for something specific or just browsing.
