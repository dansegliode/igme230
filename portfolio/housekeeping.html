Housekeeping

INTRO

In this 3 week design sprint, myself and my partner designed an Apple TV application that helps families manage household chores.

USER STORY

“When its time to clean up the house, I want to set up the TV with a chore and task list the whole family can use, so that I can curb procrastination and focus on accomplishing chores for myself and my family members.”

INTERACTION ARCHITECTURE

The family will gather and a parent will open the app and assign the family chores for the night. Family members will be able to check off their chores with the TV remote as they complete them.

SOLUTION

We wanted to design an interface that is fun to interact with and has playful illustrations, as well as a simple and easy to use interface that the whole family can enjoy. We also wanted users to feel compelled to interact with it so we designed it in 3d to give more dimension and tactility.
