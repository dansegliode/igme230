Thirsty Product Commerical

INTRO

The prompt for this piece was to make a product commercial. I chose Jeffree Star's eyeshadow palette, "Thirsty".

MOOD & STYLE

Since the concept surrounding the palette is summer and popsicles, I wanted to keep the colors bright and similar to the shades in the palette. I also wanted the secondary content in the scenes to be reminiscent of summer items, like pool tubes and bubbles.

SKETCHES

I planned out initial shots and transitions to the music in thumbnail sketches before building assets and animating.

STYLEFRAMES

I wanted the animation as a whole to be driven by and reflect the lighthearted nature of the palette and brand.
