CHALLENGES

The most challenging part of designing this site was getting the fade in and out to happen on page load. I wanted to offset when each nav item faded in, and have the body fade in and out when changing the page. I found a good solution for both by searching on codepen and stack overflow. The nav fade in targets each item in the nav and delays them so they display one after the other. The rest of the page fading in stops the default response of clicking a link, fades the body first, and then switches pages. Once I found what I was looking for I had to figure out how to have them integrate with the code I already had.

Another more constant challenge I had was making sure the pages looked like the ones I designed beforehand in Figma, and making sure nothing broke when resizing the browser. With a lot of tweaking I got everything to a point where I was happy with how it looked and responded.
